// FAQ

Common questions.

Does it work with DocuSign eSignature?
Yes. DocuFill runs on DocuSign (docusign.com and docusign.net). Open any envelope or document where you can fill fields — the extension will inject and let you map and fill.
Is my data sent to any server?
No. Your CSV and templates stay in Chrome’s local storage on your device. Nothing is uploaded to DocuFill or any third party.
What file formats are supported?
CSV (.csv) and Excel (.xlsx, .xls). The first row is used as column headers; columns are auto-detected for mapping.
